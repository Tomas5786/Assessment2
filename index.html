As can be seen from each team member's test results we cover a broad range of personalities. This should help maintain balance as each of the team members finds their rightful position within the group. It will also allow for the group to figure out a structure that best suits all members without being challenged by other members with the same personality types.

Learning Styles

The team leans towards the tactile learning style this means that they are very hands on learners needing to get their hands dirty to better understand and remember new information. The team also has Visual and Auditory teammates who better learn by watching and listening. This will help us all fit together as the tactile people learn by doing, we will also be able to show and explain it to the other team members helping them remember the new information as well.

Managment Styles

Each member of the team is spread out across two main management styles; Servant Leaders who put others first and have great delight when seeing their teammates grow but they are also the ones that give out the support needed while uploading their ethics, and Contrarian leader who are independent, have realistic work expectations and delegate decisions to other team members they are also the ones who think outside the box. Each of those management styles have 2 members Christopher and John and Oliver and Adrien respectively. Jamie is a Metamodern leader focused on the future and employees his imagination to his work while also innovating new ways to get the work done. Tomas is a Transformative Leader and shares the success with all his teammates but stays away from being the centre of attention, He also brings change to the team by fostering curiosity in the work they are doing.

End Results

Going through each team member's first assessment pages and looking at the results for 3 personality quizzes I can see these have been very helpful to a lot of the team members. They have read through and have linked very similar traits in themselves. Nearly all team members have a better understanding of their personality after completing the Myers-Briggs test which has led to the team being able to resolve issues when they arise quickly and to the advantage of the group. We are a lucky group as we don’t have many team members that have similar personalities enabling us to be able to draw on the strengths of each other whilst also knowing that we won't be stepping in the way of our teammates when completing jobs.
